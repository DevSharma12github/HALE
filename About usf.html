HALE

About Company Page


Hale is INDIA's first online platform which makes it easier for people to access food and medical facilities across highways . It provides users the option to explore restaurants and eateries along their location on a highway and then place an order to pick-up or dine-in. In cases of accidents or casualties , Hale connects the users to the closest medical organisations by sending alerts . Users can then get in touch with proper authorities to book an ambulance and notify the nearest hospital within a single click . Users can provide feedback on the services they use by writing reviews. There is also a procedure for restaurants / hospitals to partner with Hale to deliver services and boost exposure. Hale aims to enhance highway experiences by bridging the gaps in the food and medical services.
